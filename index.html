Our Sentiment Methodology

Step 1: Real-Time Intelligence

Our engine continuously monitors the global news cycle, extracting high-impact headlines specifically related to corporate earnings, regulatory shifts, and expansion news.

Step 2: Expert Contextualization

Unlike basic keyword scanners, our AI acts as a Specialized iGaming Analyst. It evaluates news through the lens of industry-specific metrics like Hold Percentage, Market Handle, and Gaming Tax Jurisdictions.

Step 3: Nuance Detection

The system detects subtle financial signals. For example, it recognizes that an "Earnings Beat" coupled with "Lowered Guidance" is actually a bearish signal, providing a more accurate score than traditional sentiment tools.

The Scoring Spectrum (0-100)

60-100 (Bullish): Strong positive momentum, earnings beats, or favorable regulation.
40-59 (Neutral): Mixed news cycle or purely administrative updates.
0-39 (Bearish): Regulatory headwinds, missed targets, or analyst downgrades.
